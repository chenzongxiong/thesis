\section{Comparison of random walk generated by LSTM networks and HNN}\label{appendix:comparsion-random-walk}

We present multiple plots generated by LSTM networks and HNN with different initial random seeds. Comparing \myfigref{fig:appendix:market-lstm-results} and \myfigref{fig:appendix:market-hnn-results}, we see that LSTM networks may produce good random walk with some specific random seed (see \myfigref{fig:appendix:market-lstm-results-8}). However, the predictive random walks generated by HNN are much more robust than LSTM networks. That confirms the results shown in \mytableref{tbl:chapter5:simulation-stock-results}.
